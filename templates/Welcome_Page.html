Welcome To SnapBlend!

This is an app that lets you add photos and make a slideshow out of them!.

You need to create a free account and login with it to use the app.

Use the links below to either sign up (if you dont already have an account) or sign in to an existing account.

After logging in and going to the upload images section, you can drag and drop images onto the provided area to upload images to your database

Then, to create a video, go to the "Create Video" section of the navbar to select photos from your database and make a viedeo out of them !!
